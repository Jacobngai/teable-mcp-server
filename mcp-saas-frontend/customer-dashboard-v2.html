Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create reminders?

Simply tell Claude: "Remind me to [task] on [date] at [time]". Claude will create a record in your Reminders table automatically.

When are reminders sent?

Reminders are checked every 5 minutes. When the date and time match, the reminder is sent to your WhatsApp.

What if I disconnect WhatsApp?

Reminders will not be sent until you reconnect. Your pending reminders will remain in the table.
