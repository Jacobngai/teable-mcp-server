Questions from Insurance Agents

My agency already provides a CRM. Why do I need this?

Agency CRMs track company metrics - policy numbers, premiums, commissions. They don't track relationships - birthdays, family details, personal notes. This tracks what YOUR clients care about.

Can I track policy details and renewal dates?

Yes! Just tell AI "Ahmad has medical card RM200/mo, renewal March 15" - it remembers. Ask "renewals this month" anytime and get the full list.

Will this work with my existing client list?

Yes! We help you import existing data. Or start fresh and add as you go. Most agents add 50-100 clients in their first week just from talking to AI.

Is my client data private?

100% private. Only YOU can access your data. We don't see it. Your agency doesn't see it. Everything encrypted.

I'm not tech-savvy. Can I still use this?

If you can send a WhatsApp voice message, you can use this. Just talk naturally - "Met Ahmad today, interested in medical" - AI handles the rest.

How is this different from just using notes in my phone?

Notes don't answer questions. You can't ask your notes "who has birthday this week?" or "show me clients who mentioned education plans." AI finds patterns you'd never see.
